Spirit-Filled Worship & Casual Dress

Our worship is contemporary, Spirit-led and Christ-centred — a mix of modern worship songs and timeless hymns. Expect a warm, genuine atmosphere where people freely worship however feels natural to them.

What should I wear?

Come as you are — seriously. Jeans, shorts, smart casual — whatever you're comfortable in. There is absolutely no dress code at Deep Waters.

A Safe Place for Your Kids

We believe kids should love church! Our Children's Ministry runs every Sunday during the service for children from birth through to Year 6.

Children check in at the Kids desk at the entrance when you arrive. Our team will walk you through the process on your first visit.

Coffee & Connection

After every service we gather for coffee, tea and a chat. This is one of our favourite parts of Sunday — a chance to meet people, ask questions, and simply enjoy community.

Our welcome team will be looking out for first-time visitors. We'd love to say hello, answer any questions, and make sure you feel genuinely at home.

What About Communion?

We celebrate Communion (the Lord's Supper) regularly as a church family. It is open to all who have put their faith in Jesus Christ. Elements are passed around and we take it together as a community. First-timers are always welcome to observe.

Parking & Getting Here

We have free onsite parking available right at the venue. Additional street parking is also available nearby. We're located at 121 Orchard Road, Doreen VIC 3754 — easy to find with GPS.
