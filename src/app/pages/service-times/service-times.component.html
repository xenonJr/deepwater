How to Find Us

By Car

We have free onsite parking right at the venue with plenty of spaces. Additional street parking is available on Orchard Road. Follow GPS to 121 Orchard Road, Doreen VIC 3754.

Public Transport

Doreen is serviced by bus routes from South Morang and Mernda train stations. Check PTV for current timetables. Feel free to contact us if you need a lift — we're happy to help.

When You Arrive

Doors open at 9:50am. Our welcome team will be at the entrance to greet you and help with anything you need. Grab a coffee, find a seat, and make yourself at home.

Ready to Join Us?

We'd love to meet you this Sunday. Let us know you're coming and we'll make sure you're looked after.
