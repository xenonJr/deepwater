How Deep Waters Began

Deep Waters Church was founded on a simple conviction — that people in Doreen and the surrounding communities deserve a church that is genuinely warm, biblically grounded, and Spirit-led.

We started as a small gathering of families with a shared desire to see God move in our neighbourhood. From those humble beginnings, God has been faithful to grow us into a diverse, multigenerational community united around Jesus Christ.

The name Deep Waters comes from Proverbs 20:5 — "The purposes of a person's heart are deep waters." We believe every person carries God-given potential waiting to be drawn out through community, discipleship, and encounter with Jesus.

To Know Jesus.
To Make Him Known.

We exist to help people encounter Jesus, grow in authentic faith, and discover the life-giving hope found in God's Word — and to take that hope into every corner of our community.

Part of a Larger Family

Deep Waters Church is proudly affiliated with Australian Full Gospel Churches — a movement of Spirit-filled, Christ-centred churches committed to building healthy congregations and reaching Australia with the Gospel.

This affiliation gives us accountability, resources, training, and connection to a wider network of churches who share our values and mission.
