Smaller Groups, Deeper Community

Home Groups are at the heart of how we do life together. Meeting mid-week in homes around the Doreen area, these smaller circles are where real friendships are built, questions are asked freely, and faith grows through everyday life.

Groups are open to all — whether you're new to faith or have walked with Jesus for years. Simply reach out and we'll connect you with a group near you.

Serving Our Community

Our Outreach Ministry takes the love of Jesus into the streets, homes and hearts of Doreen and surrounds. We partner with local initiatives, serve the vulnerable, and share hope with those who need it most.

If you have a heart for your community and want to make a real difference, Outreach is the place to start.
